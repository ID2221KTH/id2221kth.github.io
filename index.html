In this course, we describe the critical technology trends that are enabling cloud computing and the services and applications they offer. The course covers a wide variety of advanced topics in data intensive computing, including distributed file systems, NoSQL databases, processing data-at-rest (batch data) and data-in-motion (streaming data), graph processing, and resource management.
